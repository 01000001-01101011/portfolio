Modelling

On this page, you will find some models I created myself with no external models, textures, or materials. Materials are either procedurally generated in Blender or using textures that I created myself. Here is a small sample of projects I have worked on. Move the slider to view the models.

The Hound

This was my first proper experiment using armatures for character posing. This robot was designed after The Hound from the novel Fahrenheit 541. The properties and details of the creature are from the novel.
